Who is Doo-Wan Kinumbe

Congolese by nationality, Doo-Wan Kinumbe a second year student at Cavendish university Uganda pursuing Bachelors of Science in Information Technology, hereby contesting for the guild Presidency position at Cavendish university.

Self driven, I am dynamic, result-oriented and self-driven person, presentable and articulate with the respect of deadlines set by hierarchy. I am a team player and possess leadership skills and qualities to manage a multi-functional and disciplinary team.
